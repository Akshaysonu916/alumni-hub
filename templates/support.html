📚 Frequently Asked Questions

📝 How do I register as an alumnus?

Go to the Alumni Registration page and fill in your graduation and job details.

🔐 I forgot my login password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow instructions to reset it via email.

💼 How can I post a job opportunity?

Log in and go to the "Job Board" section, then click "Post a Job".

👥 Who can view my profile?

Only registered users (alumni, students, admins) based on access levels.

📅 How do I attend or register for events?

Visit the "Events" section in your dashboard and register for available events.
